Pizza 🍕

Recipe Description

This is a simple homemade pizza recipe with a crispy base, flavorful sauce, cheese & toppings. Pizza is really fun to prepare and customize according to your preferences. Start with a soft dough, spread tomato sauce, add cheese and finish with vegetables or meats if you like. The result is a fresh, hot and delicious Pizza right from kitchen.

Ingredients:

For the Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3/4 cup warm water

For the Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tsp garlic (chopped or paste)
  • 1/2 oregano
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt to taste

For Toppings

  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • Sliced onion, capsicum, tomato, or mushroom
  • Optional: olives, corn, jalapeños,cooked chicken, or sausage

Steps to Cook:

1. Prepare the Dough

  1. In warm water, mix yeast, sugar, and let it rest for 10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead into soft dough.
  3. cover and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.

2. Make the Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  2. Add garlic, sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add tomato puree, chili flakes, oregano, and salt.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes until thick.

3. Shape the Base

  1. Preheat over to 200ºC./li>
  2. Roll out the dough into a round base.
  3. Place it on a balking tray or pan.

4. Assemble the pizza

  1. Spread the sauce evenly on the base.
  2. Add grated cheese.
  3. Place your favorite toppings.
  4. Sprinkle more cheese on top (for extra flavour).

5. Bake and Serve

  1. Bake in preheated oven for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  2. slice hot and enjoy!

Now it's ready- time to enjoy your meal!
